Here is the initial gear list for [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Annapurna_Circuit][Annapurna Circuit]] keeping in mind that we will experience multiple climates:
- hot and humid - ~30째 - below 2000m (Pokhara)
- temperate - ~20째 - 2000-4000m (most of the circuit)
- mountain - ~10째 - 4000-5000m (after Manang, Tilicho lake)
- freezing level - ~0째 - above 5000m (Thorung La pass)
